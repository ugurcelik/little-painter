Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 21, 2026

Little Painter is a coloring app for children and families. We design the app to be simple, safe, and respectful of family privacy.

Developer

Little Painter is published by Ugur Celik.

Information Children Provide

Little Painter does not require children to create an account. We do not ask children to provide a name, email address, phone number, precise location, or other personal information inside the app.

Data Collection Summary

Data type Collected by Little Painter? Purpose
Name, email, phone number No Not requested in the app.
Precise location No Not used.
Photos or drawings uploaded to a server No Artwork stays on the device unless the user saves or shares it using device features.
Purchase status Yes, through Apple and RevenueCat To unlock premium content and restore purchases.

Photos and Gallery Access

The app may request access to the photo library only so a child or parent can save finished artwork to the device gallery. The app does not upload drawings or photos to our servers.

Purchases

In-app purchases are processed by Apple. Purchase status may be managed through RevenueCat so the app can unlock the correct premium content and restore purchases. RevenueCat may process an app user identifier, product identifier, transaction status, and receipt information for purchase validation. We do not receive your payment card details.

Advertising and Tracking

Little Painter does not show third-party ads and does not use third-party advertising tracking.

Analytics

Little Painter does not currently use third-party analytics for child behavior tracking. If analytics are added in the future, this policy will be updated before release.

Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe your child has provided personal information, please contact us and we will help remove it.

Data Retention

Drawings saved inside the app or to the photo library remain on the user's device. Purchase records are retained by Apple and RevenueCat as needed to provide purchases and restore access.

International Users

Little Painter may be available in multiple countries. Purchase processing and app distribution may involve Apple and RevenueCat systems outside your country.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy when the app changes. The updated date at the top of this page will reflect the latest version.

Contact
